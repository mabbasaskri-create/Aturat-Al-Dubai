Our Story

The Art of Luxury Fragrance

Aturat Al Dubai was founded with a singular vision — to bring the finest collection of premium imported perfumes, Arabic attars, and oud to the heart of Lahore, Pakistan. What began as a small passion project in the bustling lanes of Al Madina Road has blossomed into one of the most trusted names in luxury fragrances. Our journey started with a deep love for the art of perfumery and a desire to make authentic, world-class scents accessible to every fragrance enthusiast in Pakistan.

Over the past five years, we have proudly served thousands of satisfied customers who trust us for original, long-lasting perfumes at affordable prices. Our carefully curated collection features handpicked fragrances from the world's most renowned perfume houses, along with traditional Arabic attars and rare oud that transport you to the mystique of the Middle East. Whether you are looking for a bold statement scent for special occasions or a subtle everyday fragrance, Aturat Al Dubai has something to match every personality and mood — all while keeping your budget in mind.

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To bring the world's finest fragrances to Pakistan at prices everyone can enjoy. We believe that luxury perfumery should be accessible to all, not just a privileged few. Through careful curation, direct sourcing, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs, we are committed to delivering an unmatched fragrance experience right to your doorstep.

Where We Are Going

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted and beloved fragrance destination. We envision a future where every person in Pakistan can access authentic, premium-quality perfumes and attars without compromise. Through innovation, customer-centric service, and unwavering commitment to quality, we aim to redefine the fragrance shopping experience across the nation.
